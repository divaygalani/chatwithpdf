Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Veda PDF?

Veda PDF is a chatbot that allows users to upload PDF documents and interact with the content by asking questions and chatting with the bot.

How do I upload a PDF?

To upload a PDF, go to the upload section of the chatbot interface and select the PDF file you want to upload from your device.

Can I ask questions about any PDF?

Yes, you can ask questions about the content of any PDF you upload, and the chatbot will provide answers based on the information in the document.

What is the maximum size of a PDF I can upload?

The maximum file size for uploading a PDF is 50MB. If your file exceeds this limit, consider compressing it before uploading.

Is my uploaded PDF secure?

Yes, Veda PDF ensures that your uploaded documents remain private and secure. Files are not shared with third parties and are only used for answering your queries.

Can I use Veda PDF on my phone?

Yes, Veda PDF works on both desktop and mobile devices, so you can use it anytime, anywhere.
