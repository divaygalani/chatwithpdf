Cookies Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve user experience, remember preferences, and analyze website traffic.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance website functionality and user experience
  • Authenticate users via Google OAuth & GitHub Login
  • Store session data for chat history (if enabled)
  • Analyze site traffic with Google Analytics
  • Ensure website security and fraud prevention

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core website functionality (login, chat storage).
  • Performance Cookies: Used for analytics and website optimization.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences and settings.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Used for authentication (Google/GitHub), analytics, and chatbot services.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

To manage cookies:

  • Chrome: Manage Cookies
  • Firefox: Manage Cookies
  • Edge: Manage Cookies
  • Safari: Manage Cookies

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Please check this page for the latest version.
